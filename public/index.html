You are not alone!

This is a supportive story-sharing community focused on socially stigmatized issues, such as HIV, Homelessness, and Domestic Violence. People suffering from these mental/physical problems can often feel isolated and depressed. So this community provides a safe-space where users can help other users across the world by anonymously sharing their heart-warming stories around the same issue.
You know why? Because no one deserves to feel alone!

Note: This website is NOT an alternative to therapy and counseling, etc. Nor is it supposed to be that way. It is just a welcoming and friendly environment to make the social stigmas around health issues easier to handle. Also, encouraging to get help from professionals.
